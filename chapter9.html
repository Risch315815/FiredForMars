Chapter 9: Back to the Start

Act II

[ Lights up on Room 6B, the shared dorm now caught in limbo
Brendan and Changan are gathering the item that belong to each of them, placing them at their respective corner: Brendan's is on the working desk next to the console, Changan's is on a cozy 3 layered shelf.
They moved around the room in perfect harmony, like they always do.
But this time, slightly avoiding each other. ]

<the things we brought>

(verse 1)
Ch:

It's like the wrinkled part of me through time that never seem to dry

(headphone)
It was the first time that paradise gave out a call
No more lies I arrived I was home

But I look back on myself and there's still nothing
How can I fly if I can't see tomorrow?

[ Brendan and Changan nearly bumped into each other, but they stopped themselves before contact. Music stops abruptly. A short period of silence. ]

(Take me home, Country Roads Vinyl)

Act III

[ Stage splits into two living spaces:
- Changan's new standard unit: Beige wall, living room area is separated from the wall with a wall of frosted glass. The kitchen has an middle-sized oven, but no island.
- Brendan's upgraded suite. Snow-white wall with grey carpet. There's a study separating living room area and bedroom area. The kitchen has a faux-marble island, and the terrace looks down on the whole colony.
It was nice, but way too quiet, like a ghost town.
3 a.m. and they can't sleep. They lie on their bed, eyes wide open. Brendan was too bored, so he started humming a short melody. Changan did the same. Then they started humming the tune of the background music to this song. ]
