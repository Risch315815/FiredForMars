Chapter 12: Into the Abyss

The Chief Director of the colony was frowning so hard his head hurt.

Hertz again.

Earth was already sniffing around for inefficiencies and compliance slips. A same-sex intimacy scandal between two high-ranking officers? The kind of attention no one could afford.

He flagged the file and forwarded it to his trash bin. I mean deputy director.

Youssef was in the middle of reviewing a request for power grid renewal when the file landed on his terminal. He took a quick peek.
"Oh, hell no!"
He opened it with the rage of a thousand sun.

Brendan Hertz and Changan Wang. Same-sex intimacy caught on surveillance camera, red flagged.
He could rip the cock right off Brendan Hertz, but he just smashed the framed photo of him and Brendan against the wall instead.

Youssef collapsed onto his chair, rubbing his temples.
He still had at least 12 hours worth of work in queue, and 95% of Mars personnel were single men. It would be impossible to stop this kind of case from flooding in.
Moreover, Earth politics were a landmine field: if he went soft, he'd be branded negligent.
If he punished them harshly, he could be cancelled for being a homophobe. Youssef felt chills down his spine.
Worst of all, Brendan was good to him. He knew he should be impartial, but he just couldn't. Also, he didn't want to be seen as ungrateful.

Another uphill battle.

He dug up the original clause: "No intimacy between same-sex personnel."
"It was actually an executive order from Director Claudius? Isn't that guy a known homophobe and tyrant?"

Youssef felt unease, paged through flagged cases. He was baffled by the statistics:
72% of reported officers had been model employees at least once; up to 42% of reported officers were falsely-claimed, or are retaliated.
Youssef knew there would be corruption, but not like this.
34% of reported officers were convicted and reassigned to remote posts. Half of those reassignments caused endless rank changes, which lead to endless chaos.

Youssef knew the system could not be trusted, but this is on a whole new level.

He submitted a request to Earth:
"I, Deputy Director of Mars Colony, Youssef Ahsan, suggest a revision on Mars Executive Order clause 211: 'No intimacy between same-sex personnel.'

The rationale is as follow:
First, this clause disproportionately targets visible, competent personnel, often for retaliatory purposes. Its enforcement causes great disruption in the chain of command, destabilizing the system.
Second, the clause originates from an infamous chief director of the Mars colony, whose doing such as excessive enforcement and homophobia is best left forgotten.

I suggest that the clause be revised as: 'No public displays of intimacy between same-sex personnel.'"

Three days later, Earth responded.
"Amended language approved. Retroactive application permitted for special-case scenarios."
Youssef filed it, and forwarded the closure form.

He picked up the frame on the other side of the room, took out the photo, rubbed it into a ball, and threw it in the trash.

Brendan and Changan were released without even a punishment.
Their personal items were returned to them in a boutique box. Changan suspect the surface of his phone has been particularly cleaned.
The guards were extra polite to them on their way out. Changan was uneasy, while Brendan's steps turned into strides.

Brendan reached out for Changan's hand.
Changan stepped away.

That night, Changan was at home editing propaganda footage he left off 3 days ago.

The door opened.

Brendan strolled in, whistling, chipper.
"Guess who's free~"

Changan didn't look up.
"I'm working."

Unfazed, Brendan padded into the kitchen, pulled down the burlap bag, poured the leaves into the pot.
He let it steep, then carried it to the living room table and poured two cups.

Changan finally finished his work. He dragged his feet into the living room area.
"Come on. Sit." Brendan patted the couch beside him.
Changan sighed and sat down.

Brendan beamed. "Hey, be happy."
Changan murmured, "Like I have a choice."
"What?"
"Nothing."

Brendan was relentless.
"I can't believe we never opened it the whole time we were together." He laughed. "But we won! I guess there's no better time to open it."
He poured a cup and shoved it into Changan's face. "Come on! Have a taste!"

Changan set the cup down. "I'm fine."
Brendan was slightly disappointed, but he kept trying. "Well, I know a way to cheer you up~"
He reached his hand between Changan's legs.

Changan slapped it away.
"Stop it! Whatever you're doing! Just stop!"

Brendan blinked. "Babe, what's wrong?"
"YOU are what's wrong! You think you know everything! I don't want any of this, you keep pushing and I didn't know how to resist!"
Brendan was shocked. "How can you say this? I worked so hard. I did it all for us!"
"No!" Changan yelled. "You did it all for YOU! I am just an escort so you don't feel bored on your adventure!"
He breathed in before he shouted out: "You are a hopeless, selfish jerk!"

Brendan's face twisted. Hurt turned fast to fury.
"Whatever, ching chang!"
Then he walked out, slamming the door behind him.

Changan sat quiet on the couch, watched as the cup of tea turned cold.
